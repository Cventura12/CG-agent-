# Willingness To Pay Check-In

This is the Day 28 script for the first pricing signal check-in.

## Rules

- Do the check-in with each beta contractor by:
  - in person
  - or phone
- Do not do it by text.
- Ask only the three questions below.
- Do not pitch while they are answering.
- Write down their exact words.

## Three Questions Only

Ask these in order:

1. `What does it save you?`
2. `What is still annoying?`
3. `Would you pay for it - and if so, what would you pay?`

After you ask, stop talking.

Do not explain pricing tiers.
Do not defend the product.
Do not try to "help" them answer.

## What To Capture

Log verbatim:

- what they say it saves
- what still annoys them
- whether they would pay
- the exact number or range they mention
- any hesitation before they answer

The exact wording matters for positioning and pricing.

## Payment Signal

If even one contractor gives a real willingness-to-pay signal:

- any dollar amount
- any monthly number
- any "I'd pay if it did X"

That is meaningful signal.

Offer a payment link the same day.

## Payment Link Offer

Use a simple payment-link tool you can create immediately:

- Stripe Payment Links
- Square Payment Links
- PayPal payment link

You do not need subscriptions on day one.
You need a clean way to test whether they will actually pay.

## Exact Follow-Up Line

If they say they would pay, use:

`I can send you a simple payment link today so we can keep this in the beta and tighten it around your workflow.`

Then send it that day.

## Success Standard

Day 28 is only complete when:

- all 3 contractors have been asked the 3 questions
- responses are logged verbatim in [BETA_LOG.md](c:/Users/caleb/OneDrive/Desktop/hello/BETA_LOG.md)
- if anyone expressed willingness to pay, a payment link was offered
