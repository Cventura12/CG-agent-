# First Beta Contractor Onboard

This is the Day 23 field script and checklist for the first in-person beta onboarding.

## Target

- Go in person.
- Primary targets from the current plan:
  - Lee Roofing
  - Tallent Roofing
- Do not cold email first.

## Opening Script

Use this exact script and stop talking:

`I'm a local developer building a tool that generates roofing quotes from a voice note. Takes about 10 minutes to set up. I'd like to watch you use it on a real job and tell me what's wrong. There's no cost and no obligation.`

Do not over-explain unless they ask a direct question.

## What To Ask For

- Ask for the person who handles estimating or sales quotes.
- If the front desk asks what you need:
  - say you are showing a roofing quote tool for whoever prices jobs.

## If They Agree

Do same-day setup before you leave.

1. Open the app on their phone.
2. Confirm they can reach the deployed beta URL.
3. Enter or confirm their beta contractor account.
4. Test one short voice note.
5. Generate one real quote.
6. Tap `Send PDF`.
7. Make sure the share sheet opens on their device.

Do not schedule a second visit for basic setup if they are willing now.

## What To Watch

Do not coach them unless they are completely stuck.

Write down:

- what they say before they touch the screen
- where they hesitate
- what they expected to tap first
- whether they use voice or switch to typing
- what job details they include that you did not expect
- where the quote looks wrong to them
- whether the PDF looks acceptable to them
- whether they trust the price enough to send it

## Observation Log

Use this exact template during the visit:

- Contractor:
- Company:
- Date:
- Device:
- Mobile carrier / connection:
- Real job used:
- Did they complete `voice -> quote -> PDF -> send`?
- First thing they tried:
- First place they got confused:
- What they asked for that does not exist yet:
- What they ignored that you thought mattered:
- Quote issue(s) they pointed out:
- PDF issue(s) they pointed out:
- Exact words they used when something felt wrong:
- Would they use it again this week? Yes / No
- Why:

## Leave-Behind

Before you leave:

1. Leave them with [beta_onboarding.md](c:/Users/caleb/OneDrive/Desktop/hello/docs/beta_onboarding.md)
2. Write your phone number on the copy or message it to them directly
3. Tell them to text you any time:
   - a quote is wrong
   - a voice note gets misread
   - a PDF looks unprofessional
   - they get stuck and cannot finish in under 2 minutes

## Success Standard

Day 23 is only complete when:

- at least one contractor has the app on their phone
- they attempt one real quote
- you capture notes on what broke or felt off
